Welcome to Cumilla

Experience the history, culture, and delicious food of Cumilla!

Cumilla is a vibrant city located in the southeastern part of Bangladesh, rich in both historical significance and natural beauty. Known for its ancient Buddhist heritage, Cumilla is home to several archaeological sites, including the famous Shalban Vihara and Somapura Mahavihara. The city offers a mix of historical landmarks and modern urban growth, making it an ideal destination for both culture and progress. Apart from its rich history, Cumilla is also famous for its delicious local cuisine. With its welcoming community and scenic beauty, Cumilla stands as an important cultural hub in Bangladesh.

  2. Dharmasagor Par
